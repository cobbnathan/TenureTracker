Book — keep in mind
  • More runway now. Finishing the proposal this fall gives ~6 months of buffer before tenure review.
  • Start drafting before you hear back. Submit in November, open Chapter 1 in December.
  • Under contract ≠ published. Your tenure committee needs a press letter confirming contract and expected pub date.
  • Peer review is out of your hands. The buffer in this timeline is largely for absorbing delays there.
  • Write weekly, not in bursts. Consistent 2–3 hour sessions beat marathon breaks.
Grant strategy — keep in mind
  • ACLS 2-year lapse rule. Cannot apply within 2 years of ending any supported research leave. Plan around sabbaticals deliberately.
  • NEH topic restriction. Cold War France/Finland fits "Western civilization" currently — reconfirm scope each cycle.
  • Finnish funders favor local affiliation. Kone and Finnish Cultural Foundation both prefer a Finnish host institution.
  • Don't take leave-funded fellowship the year before your semester sabbatical — protects ACLS eligibility.
  • AMS subvention: Apply near Book 1's publication contract, not before.
